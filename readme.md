The Nike Sales dataset encapsulates 9,360 sales transactions across various U.S. regions from January 1, 2020, to December 31, 2021. Structured in CSV format, it details product types, sales methods, and retailer information. Key variables include Invoice Date, Product, Region, Retailer, Sales Method, State, Price per Unit, Total Sales, and Units Sold. 

Ideal for trend analysis and market research, this dataset serves as a resource for understanding consumer behavior, regional sales performance, and product popularity within the Nike retail landscape.
